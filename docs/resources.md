# Official IREB Resources

All official documents are free to download from the [IREB Download Center](https://cpre.ireb.org/en/downloads-and-resources/downloads).

## Foundation Level Documents

| Document | Version | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **CPRE-FL Syllabus** | v3.3.0 (April 2026) | Defines the Educational Objectives and content for the Foundation Level certification exam. The authoritative reference for what is tested. |
| **CPRE-FL Handbook** | v1.3.1 (English) | Complements the syllabus with additional explanations and examples. Does not replace a training course. |
| **CPRE Glossary** | v2.2.0 (English) | Standardized term definitions. All foundational terms must be known for the exam, even if not explicitly listed in the Educational Objectives. Available in 13 languages. |
| **Practice Exam** | Set_Public_EN_v3.3.1 | 45 questions in the same format as the live exam. Includes solutions. Available in 12 languages and as an online self-assessment. |
| **Examination Regulations** | v5.6.2 | Specifies exam duration, structure, question types with examples, and special regulations. Covers both Foundation Level and RE@Agile Primer. |
| **Nine Fundamental Principles** | v1.0.0 | Introduction to the nine principles that form the foundation of Requirements Engineering (covered in Educational Unit 2 of the syllabus). |
| **RE Poster** | v1.0.0 | Visual reference summarizing Requirements Engineering concepts. |

## Recommended Study Order

1. Download the **Syllabus** and **Handbook** first — use them alongside this study guide
2. Use the **Glossary** to look up any unfamiliar term as you study
3. After completing all chapters, take the official **Practice Exam** under timed conditions
4. Review the **Examination Regulations** to understand question types and scoring before exam day
